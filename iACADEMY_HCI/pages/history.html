iACADEMY TIMELINE

Ayala Campus

iACADEMY opens its doors and becomes the only college along Ayala avenue.

2002

CHED

iACADEMY's program was granted Zero Deficiency by the Commission on Higher Education.

WACOM

iACADEMY is the first college that is a WACOM Authorized Training Partner.

2009

IBM

Designated as the first IBM Center of Excellence in the ASEAN region.

2009

Project Runway Philippines

iACADEMY opened its fashion design and technology program and was chosen by Solar Entertainment as the official partner school of Project Runway Philippines.

2010

Weather Philippines

iACADEMY teams up with Weather Philippines to develop the website and application for faster real-time weather updates.

2011

DePaul University

DePaul University, the largest Catholic school in America, engages with iACADEMY for study link partnership.

2012

iACADEMY Plaza in Buendia

iACADEMY moves to its new home in Buendia.

2013

POLIMODA

Fashion design school Polimoda (Italy) becomes one of iACADEMY's international partners for study tour programs.

2013

Microsoft Training Partner

Students from the School of Computing can now become Microsoft Certified Professionals even before they graduate.

2014

ACPI

iACADEMY hosts Animahenasyon the largest animation festival in the country.

2014
Note: This timeline was retrieved from the current iACADEMY Website 2015.
