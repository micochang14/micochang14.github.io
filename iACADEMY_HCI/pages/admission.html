Application Procedure Step-By-Step Guide


Complete application form and requirements for admission and prepare a non-refundable application fee of 200php.


Schedule the preferred date of the examination by connecting with Facebook on the iACADEMY website.


Take the exam and wait for the results. Interview will be scheduled.


Pay the 10,000php reservation fee and submit all requirements along with the Original Test Results Report - signed and stamped "For Enrollment".
